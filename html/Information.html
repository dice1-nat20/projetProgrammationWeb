Enemies Of Democracy

To ensure the survival of Managed Democracy, every Helldiver must recognize the scum that threatens our way of life. The galaxy is currently plagued by three primary factions: the mindless, communist Automatons who seek to replace liberty with cold logic; the tyrannical Terminids, a plague of space-borne insects driven by pure expansionist greed; and the mind-controlling Illuminates, ancient manipulators who use psychic subterfuge to enslave the weak-willed. Knowledge is your first weapon. Study them, then destroy them.
