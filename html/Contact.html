Clarification

The theme of this project is set in a fictional universe. Any resemblance to actual events or real persons would be purely coincidental.


Theme

The website we created is set in the universe of the video game Helldivers 2, developed by Arrowhead Game Studios and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment, released on February 8, 2024. It is a third-person cooperative multiplayer shooter in which teams of players complete missions across various planets. The game's universe takes place in a fictional future where humanity, spread across the galaxy, faces attacks from extraterrestrial species threatening its survival. Human society is governed by a system called "Managed Democracy", which forms the thematic and satirical core of the work. Indeed, Helldivers 2 is above all an unapologetic political satire: beneath its patriotic and heroic surface, the game depicts an authoritarian regime that sends its soldiers (the Helldivers, supposedly an "elite" force) to fight as cannon fodder across multiple simultaneous fronts. This irony is pervasive, including in the game's official communications, whose promotional videos deliberately take the form of propaganda clips from "Super Earth", the fictional governing entity. It is precisely this parodic propaganda aesthetic that we chose to adopt as the guiding thread of our website. The tone used is intentionally exaggerated and caricatural, solely to match the game's humor and bring a smile to the visitor's face. It obviously reflects no real-world opinion whatsoever, but rather serves as an exercise in world-building that remains consistent with the source material.
